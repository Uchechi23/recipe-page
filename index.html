Chicken Stew Recipe

The chicken stew is a delightful West Africa stew made with, more often than not, chicken thighs and simmered in a savoury sauce made with tomatoes and peppers. It is so vrsatile and can be eaten with varieties of dishes.

Recipe Information

  • Prep Time: 20 to 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 to 40 minutes
  • Servings: Dish with hot Nigerian Rice
  • Difficulty: Intermidiate

Ingredients:

  • Washed Chicken parts
  • Freshly blended tomatoes, Pepper with Onion
  • Tomatoes Paste
  • Freshly blended Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • About 3-4 seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Chicken seasoning spices
  • Curry and thyme
  • Freshly sliced onion
  • Freshly Grated red-peper balls

Instructions:

  1. To properly season your washed chicken parts:

    • Add your washed chicken thigh into a heating pot
    • Then add 2 seasoning cubes of your choice
    • Spice it up with a little curry and thyme powder
    • Add a little of the chicken spice powder
    • Add a pinch of salt
    • Add your freshly blended ginger and garlic paste
    • Add a little grated red bell pepper
    • Then add a little of your sliced onion
    • Stir properly till all ingredient mix up and marinate into your chicken thigh
    • Then a little water
    • Cover up the pot and let it boil for 15 to 20 minutes

      • Make sure to sort your chicken thigh out of the pot once ready, so as to have just the stock

    • Proceed to fry your chicken thigh (optional)

  2. To properly make your stew

    • Add a half-cup of your olive oil into a heating pot
    • Allow oil to heat up for 1 - 2 minutes
    • Then add your sliced onion
    • Add a little curry powder to spice the aroma
    • Let your onion fry for about a minute
    • Then add your blended tomatoes and pepper mix
    • Let it fry for about 10 to 15 minutes
    • Add the tomatoes paste afterwards
    • Let it fry for about 5 to 10 mins
    • Then add your chicken stock, the seasoned water left after boiling your chicken
    • Add 2 seasoning cubes afterwards
    • Add a pinch of salt
    • Let it fry for about 10 minutes
    • Add your already cooked chicken parts
    • Add a little water (optional)

Well, Chicken Stew offers several nutritional benefits, primarily due to its combination of protein-rich chicken and various vegetables. It can be a good source of protein, vitamins, and minerals while also providing hydration snd potentially aiding in cold and flu relief.
